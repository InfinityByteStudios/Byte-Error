◦ HOW TO PLAY ◦
• **Objective**: Survive as long as possible against waves of neural viruses
• **Combat**: Aim with mouse, click to shoot, eliminate all enemies in each wave
• **Survival**: Use Neural Dash (SPACE) to escape danger and avoid enemy attacks
• **Overclock**: Build charge by dealing damage, activate with Q for powerful transformation
• **Transformation**: During Overclock, your neural core transforms into the ultimate OverClock form
• **Safe Zones**: Enter blue safe zones for temporary protection from enemies
• **Upgrades**: Choose neural enhancements between waves to improve your capabilities
• **Strategy**: Balance aggressive combat with strategic retreats and resource management
◦ GAME INFO ◦
• **NeuroCore: Byte Wars** - A cyberpunk survival shooter game
• **Genre**: Top-down arcade shooter with roguelike progression
• **Theme**: Neural network defense against digital viruses
• **Features**: Wave-based combat, upgrade system, multiple difficulties
• **Developed by**: InfinityByte Studios
• **Version**: v1.3.0 - Neural Enhancement Update
• **Engine**: HTML5 Canvas with modern web technologies
◦ DIFFICULTY SYSTEM ◦
• **Easy 🟢**: 150 HP, weaker enemies, healing during waves
• **Medium 🟡**: 100 HP, balanced enemies, healing after waves
• **Hard 🔴**: 100 HP, tough enemies, healing every 3rd wave
• **Extreme ☠️**: 100 HP, brutal enemies, no healing ever
• **Dynamic Scaling**: Enemy stats change based on selected difficulty
• **Change Difficulty**: Press B on game over screen to switch
